# Project Roadmap

## Phase 1: Proof of Concept

- Export sample PML macro from AVEVA E3D
- Demonstrate equipment scaling by 1.5
- Preserve location and orientation
- Preserve connected pipe relationships
- Generate new unique equipment name
- Produce validation checklist

## Phase 2: Equipment Package Redesign

- Extend from one equipment item to a connected equipment package
- Handle multiple pipes and nozzles
- Add support structure considerations
- Strengthen validation rules

## Phase 3: Process Unit Redesign

- Apply AI transformations to a refinery process unit
- Support modular replication
- Support capacity scaling scenarios
- Integrate P&ID and line list logic

## Phase 4: Full Facility Redesign

- Extend capability toward full refinery layout transformation
- Support brownfield upgrade scenarios
- Enable AI-assisted concept design
- Improve digital twin readiness

## Phase 5: Industry Pilot and Commercialization

- Partner with an operator, EPC, or technology vendor
- Conduct controlled pilot
- Validate productivity and quality outcomes
- Package as SaaS, consulting solution, or engineering accelerator
