# Funding Positioning

## Positioning Statement

Twinnovative is developing an AI-driven engineering transformation framework for oil and gas facility design. The platform preserves engineering expertise from existing AVEVA E3D models and enables controlled AI-assisted redesign of refinery assets using PML macros.

## Why Fund This Project

The project addresses a major industry challenge: engineering expertise is difficult to capture, reuse, and scale. By converting existing design logic into AI-readable workflows, the solution can reduce redesign time, improve consistency, and support faster infrastructure delivery.

## Funding Use

Funding will support:

- Prototype development
- AVEVA E3D test environment
- AI model workflow development
- PML parser and transformation engine
- Engineering validation rules
- Pilot with refinery or EPC partner
- Commercialization and partnership development

## Target Partners

- Oil and gas operators
- EPC contractors
- National oil companies
- Digital engineering vendors
- Development finance institutions
- Innovation funds
- Universities and applied research centers
